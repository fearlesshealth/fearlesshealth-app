# Letter of Intent Templates

Use these to collect LOIs from clinics, health centres, or administrators.
Even 3–5 of these submitted with your grant application is strong traction evidence.

---

## Template 1 — WhatsApp / Short Message

> Hi [Name], I'm working on a free digital hospital management system for clinics in [Country].
> It handles patient records, appointment scheduling, and billing via blockchain payments.
> We're looking for partner clinics for our pilot. Would your facility be open to a 15-min demo?
> Live demo: https://your-app.vercel.app/demo

---

## Template 2 — Email (Formal)

**Subject:** Free Digital Health Records System — Partnership Opportunity

Dear [Dr./Administrator Name],

My name is [Your Name], and I am the founder of FearlessHealth, a digital hospital management
platform built specifically for clinics in [Region/Country].

We have developed a system that provides:
- Digital patient records (replacing paper files)
- Online appointment scheduling
- Billing and payment processing via Stellar blockchain
- Secure patient consent management

We are currently seeking 3–5 pilot clinics to partner with at no cost during our
launch phase. In exchange, we ask only for a brief letter of intent and feedback
on the system.

A live demo is available at: https://your-app.vercel.app/demo

Would you be available for a 15-minute call or visit this week?

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
[Phone Number]
[Email]
FearlessHealth

## Where to find clinics without connections

1. **Google Maps** — search "private clinic [your city]" — call or walk in
2. **LinkedIn** — search "hospital administrator" or "clinic manager" + your country
3. **Facebook Groups** — "Healthcare Professionals [Ghana/Nigeria/Kenya]"
4. **University health centres** — every university has one, easy to access
5. **Community pharmacies** — they often manage basic records and refer patients
6. **WhatsApp** — post in any medical professional group you're in

## What to say if they ask about cost

"The system is completely free during our pilot phase. We're funded by a
blockchain grant (Stellar Community Fund) and our goal is to prove impact,
not charge fees right now."
